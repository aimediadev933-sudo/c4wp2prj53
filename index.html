About WCNOSM

This project aimed to enhance the technical skills of campus network professionals in developing countries by conducting a five-day blended workshop on campus network operation and security management.

It addressed real-world challenges faced by university IT staff and promoted best practices in network management and cybersecurity. Participants from Bangladesh and other TEIN partner countries benefited through hands-on training, collaborative forums, and industry-led sessions.

Our Mission

To build the operational and cybersecurity capacities of NREN and university network engineers to ensure secure and sustainable campus network environments across TEIN partner countries.
